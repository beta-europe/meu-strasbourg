Contact

We have a large, international team of more than 50 volunteers, all of whom work in designated teams. Below is a list of the contactable Heads of each team:

  • To donate to MEUS, please contact Head of Fundraising Alex Baldwin at a.baldwin@beta-­europe.org.
  • To find out how to become a partner organisation of MEUS, please contact Head of the Country Liaison Team Stefan Zivkovic at s.zivkovic@beta-­europe.org.
  • If you are a prospective applicant to or accepted participant of MEUS with a query, please contact Head of Participants Coordination Claudio Teixeira at c.teixeira@beta-­europe.org or support@meu-­strasbourg.org
  • For general enquiries about MEUS, please contact Secretary Maria Glimos at m.glimos@beta-­europe.org or support@meu-­strasbourg.org.
