Wondering who’s behind all this?

Organisers

Model European Union Strasbourg is organised by more than 70 young volunteers from all parts of Europe and beyond. A great deal of us are former participants who fell in love with the project and stayed on to help make this year’s version possible.

We come from different countries, different walks of life, different studies and in the organising process we take on very different roles. What we have in common is a love for the project, and a wish to make Model European Union Strasbourg 2017 the best version yet!
