Who supports us?

Our Partners

Model European Union Strasbourg is supported by a wealth of individuals, organisations, institutions and prominent European leaders. We are as proud of the support we have received in the past from leaders such as Manuel Barroso and Herman Van Rompuy as we are of the support we receive today from figures such as Martin Schulz and Maroš Šefčovič.

An equally important source of support is our network of partners, which reach far beyond Europe’s borders. This year we more than doubled our number of partners, and we keep looking for new partners who are willing to support our cause. If that partner is you, send an email to the Head of our Country Liaison Team at c.teixeira@beta-europe.org
