Who supports us?

Our Partners

Our partners represent an invaluable part of the project and without their support MEUS could never be realized.

Every year we are supported by a wealth of individuals, organisations, institutions and European leaders. In the past we have received support from prominent leaders across EU countries and political factions among those were figures such as Manuel Barroso, Herman Van Rompuy, Martin Schulz or Jean-Claude Juncker.

An equally important source of support is our network of partners, which reaches far beyond Europe’s borders. This year we are proud to be partners of more than 90 organisations from 52 countries including well-known NGOs as well as new student associations and political think tanks. Our goal is to establish a long-term partnerships and an efficient cooperation with organisations which share the same values: fostering the European identity and democratic values and helping young people with their professional development.

Are you interested in becoming our partner? Send an email to the Head of our Country Liaison Team Kamila Polakova at k.polakova@france.beta-­europe.org
